# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s
- Maintain a professional profile
- Think before posting content
- Respect others’ opinions
- Use privacy settings properly
- Share authentic information

## ❌ Don’ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive data
- Avoid offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news
- Avoid oversharing personal life
- Do not engage in online arguments
