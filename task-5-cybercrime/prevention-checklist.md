# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Always verify sender email addresses
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Never share passwords or OTPs
- Use strong and unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Check website URLs before entering details
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Regularly update passwords

## 💰 UPI Safety
- Never approve unknown payment requests
- Do not share UPI PIN with anyone
